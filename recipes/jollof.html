Family Jollof Rice

A delicious, smoky jollof rice recipe perfect for family meals and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomatoes & peppers
  • Onions
  • Seasoning & spices
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions.
  2. Cook sauce with oil and seasoning.
  3. Add rice and stock.
  4. Cook until tender and smoky.
