Quick Breakfast Pancakes

Soft and fluffy pancakes that are quick to make — perfect for busy mornings with kids.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • Butter or oil

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add egg and milk, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat pan and lightly grease.
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form.
  5. Flip and cook until golden.

💡 Mom Tip: Add bananas or blueberries for extra nutrition!
