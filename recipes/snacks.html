Healthy Kids Snacks

Simple, nutritious snack ideas that are perfect for kids and easy for busy moms to prepare.

Snack Ideas

  • Sliced fruits with yogurt dip
  • Peanut butter and apple slices
  • Mini sandwiches with whole wheat bread
  • Boiled eggs with a pinch of salt
  • Homemade popcorn

How to Prepare

  1. Wash and cut fruits into bite-size pieces.
  2. Prepare dips or spreads ahead of time.
  3. Store snacks in containers for quick access.
  4. Serve fresh or pack for school lunches.
